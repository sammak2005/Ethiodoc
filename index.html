Ethiopia

Ethiopia is a melting pot of scenic natural wonders, fascinating history, rare wildlife, and deep-rooted identity.

It is one of the most scenically beautiful and fertile countries in Africa, which is exactly the opposite of the presumed notion of being famine and drought infected

    • 13_months_in_a_year

    Ethiopia, being one of the few countries in the world, still uses its own calendar system. An Ethiopian year is comprised of 13 months, and is seven years behind the Gregorian calendar.

    While the first 12 months have 30 days, the last month, called Pagume, has five days and six days in a leap year.

  • Warmest months are April and May ,The Coldest months are November and December .

    • Land_of_the_oldest_people

    Based on archaeological findings, the Hadar in Ethiopia might be the place, from where we originated.

    In 1974, Lucy was discovered here, a 3.2-million-year-old hominid skeleton, which makes it the oldest fossilised skeleton.

  • Hadar, a site in the Awash Valley of the Afar Triangle in Ethiopia
